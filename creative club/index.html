ABOUT US

The purpose of the creative club is to simulate interest in creative ideas among students of various departments. The club attempts to create an awareness of student mind. This programs gives an opportunity for students to develop their interactions among groups, helps enhance their creative and leadership skills. The original idea implemented by the student of Silicon Institute of Technology,Bhubaneswer. Having their own ideas Making links Choosing way to do things To encourage children to share their thoughts, ideas, and feelings through a range of activities
